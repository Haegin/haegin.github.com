About Me

  • In my spare time I tinker with computers, work on various websites (including this one), read (mainly fantasy and sci-fi but I do venture into other genres occasionally), play tabletop role-playing games (mainly D&D in the past but I've been playing more Traveller recently), go walking (proper walking, not just to the shops and back), go cycling and go diving.
